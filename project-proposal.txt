

                Subject
Personal Finance & Beginner Business Guide for Students in Nigeria

                Content
This website will provide simple and practical information to help students understand money management and how to start a small business. It will include:

Introduction to personal finance (saving, budgeting, avoiding debt)
Basics of starting a small business (ideas, planning, and capital)
Common mistakes beginners make in business and how to avoid them
Simple business ideas students can start in Nigeria
Tools such as a budget calculator and profit estimator

            Reason
I chose this topic because I am interested in improving my 
financial knowledge and learning how to build a profitable business.
Many students in Nigeria want to make money but lack guidance on managing finances and starting a business. 
This website will help provide simple and useful information while also helping me practice my web development skills.
