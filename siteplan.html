Website Plan

Finance & Business Guide for Students in Nigeria

Site Name

Finance & Business Guide

This name was chosen because it clearly represents the purpose of the website, which is to teach students how to manage money and start a business.

Site Purpose

This website provides simple and practical information to help students understand personal finance and how to start a small business. It includes tips on saving, budgeting, and business ideas.

Scenarios

  • How can I start a small business as a student with little money?
  • What are the best ways to save money as a student in Nigeria?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Blue (#1f4e79) – used for header and headings

Secondary Color: Light Gray (#f4f4f4) – used for background

Accent Color: Yellow (#f9a825) – used for highlights and buttons

Typography

Headings: Georgia (serif font)

Body Text: Arial (sans-serif font)
